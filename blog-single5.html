Physical Self vs Virtual Self

This is an art that truly represents my physical and virtual self. Let’s first talk about the colors. I personally chose black and white to represent my two halves. The white one is my physical self and the black one is my virtual self.

Let’s start off with my white side aka my physical self. White is usually represented as the color of purity, untaintedness, and clean. However, in my artwork, I represented it as the light, something very blinding that you are not able to see what’s behind or what it truly is. This light bends to different forms to accommodate for his environment. Like me, I always try to conform to others and try to fit in with everyone. I try to do what everyone says is right and what social norms are established.

As for the black side, it is usually associated with dirty, evil, and malicious. However, for this artwork it represents what I truly am. This “dark” side is my unfiltered side where I can truly express what I think, what I feel, and what I want to do. This side is always dominated by my light side but it can truly reign when I am online. This darkness will step back and know his limits but always knows how to assert himself in return.

However, with the recent happenings and the covid 19 pandemic forcing us to go online. These two sides have meshed and stirred quite vigorously. Hence the lines of black extending from the black side to the white side and vice versa. This can also be represented with the abundance of glitches and patches of black and white everywhere.
