What are the differences between M.I.T Literacy and it's effects on society

  • Kean Genota
  • Society

Media Literacy

Media Literacy is the ability to analyze, understand, and comprehend messages and themes that come with the media that is being presented. It also includes the ability to critically think about the impact and influence of media on the thoughts, emotions, behaviors and actions of the viewers.

Information Literacy

Information Literacy is the ability to efficiently use information to your advantage in all situations. This ability consists of many factors including finding, communicating, and analyzing information. This is a skill that’s very important in today’s world as we gather information in every corner of our lives.

Technology Literacy

Technology Literacy is an ability that revolves more or less around technology and the ways to use it. This includes the ability to effectively access, create, communicate, and evaluate information with the use of technology.


How does media, information, and technology affect society

Media, information, and technology has been a large part of our advancement as a society in these past few decades. With the evolution of technology, it has increased the amount of information that can be accessed through the internet, television, and other sources of information. One example is the media with the news. Before, we couldn’t access information easily and we had to buy newspapers. Now, it’s being shown in a more understandable way. Same goes with the internet. You can learn everything on it nowadays. Before, you have to find a teacher or a mentor to teach a skill to you. But now, you can just easily get it.

I think this has definitely spoiled our generation and we’re taking for granted the amount of information that’s spoon fed and available to us. We as a society have been lazing around as we know that we can access these information any time.
